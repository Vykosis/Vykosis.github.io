## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is TextbookAI?

TextbookAI lets you upload PDF textbooks and have AI-powered conversations about their content. Ask questions, get summaries, and study more effectively — all from your phone.

### What file types are supported?

TextbookAI currently supports PDF files. For best results, use PDFs with selectable text rather than scanned images.

### How long does it take to process a PDF?

Processing time depends on the size of the document. Most textbooks are processed within a few minutes. Very large files (1,000+ pages) may take longer. You'll see a progress indicator in the app.

### Is there a limit on how many PDFs I can upload?

Free accounts can upload up to 3 PDFs. The Student plan supports up to 20 PDFs, and the Power plan offers unlimited uploads. All plans include unlimited chat messages.

### How do I upgrade or manage my subscription?

Go to **Settings → Subscription** in the app to view plans and manage your subscription. Purchases are handled through your device's app store.

### Can I use TextbookAI on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same account on any device and your documents and chat history will sync automatically.

### How do I delete my account?

Go to **Settings → Delete Account** in the app. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.

### My PDF is stuck on "processing"

If a document stays in the processing state for more than 10 minutes, try deleting it and re-uploading. If the issue persists, contact us at the email above with the document name and approximate file size.

### The AI's answers seem inaccurate

TextbookAI generates responses based on the content of your uploaded PDF. Try rephrasing your question or being more specific. If you notice a consistent issue, please let us know.
