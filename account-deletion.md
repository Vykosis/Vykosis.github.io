How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and associated data directly within the TextbookAI app:

Open TextbookAI
Tap the Settings icon in the top right of your Library
Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
Confirm deletion
If you are unable to access the app, email [your email] with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

Data That Is Deleted

Your account and login credentials
All uploaded document data and text embeddings
All chat messages and conversation threads
Your subscription and usage records
Your document groups and library organization
Data That Is Retained

None. All user data is permanently deleted upon account deletion.
Retention Period

In-app deletion is processed immediately
Email-based requests are processed within 7 business days
